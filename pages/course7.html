Robotics Process Automation

Course Description

Automate repetitive, rule-based tasks using software robots (bots). RPA technology mimics human interactions with digital systems, improving efficiency, accuracy, and productivity across various business processes.

Course Modules

Module 1: Core Concepts of RPA

Understand the fundamentals of RPA, its benefits, and limitation.

Module 2: RPA Tools and Technologies

Gain hands-on experience with popular RPA tools like UiPath, Automation Anywhere, or Blue Prism.

Module 3: RPA Development Lifecycle

Learn the complete lifecycle of an RPA project, from design to deployment.

Module 4: Advanced RPA Techniques

Master advanced techniques like data extraction, API integration, and error handling.

Module 5: Real-world Applications

Explore real-world use cases of RPA and understand its impact on various industries.
